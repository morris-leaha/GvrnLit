Take Our Quiz




We are a small team of Junior developers currently attending the Northwestern's Coding Bootcamp. We created this app in-mind for making voting easier for the millennial population, following basis of a dating app, GvrnLit matches users to the most likely 2020 presidential candidate based on their views answered in our quiz. We believe that everyone should exercise their constitutional right to vote, but we also understand that politics can be overwhelming. With only roughly 60% of eligible voter turnout for the 2016 presidential election, GvrnLit hopes to encourage more individuals to vote and to vote with confidence!
